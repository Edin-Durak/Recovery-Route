How it works

RecoveryRoute works by allowing you to list your programs directly, rather than your center(s). Your programs can then be found on our marketplace and shown to users who fit your determined criteria.

Pay-per-lead pricing

Listing your programs on RecoveryRoute is completely free.

Our pay-per-lead pricing system means that you only pay a small percentage (.7%) of your total program cost to confirm bookings.

Your leads will have also paid a booking fee, so you know they have carefully decided that your program best suits their needs and are serious about checking in.
